Understand and solve the preservation problem for previously unannotated rare subpopulations during single-cell batch integration from a biological-science perspective.

## Core Responsibilities

Provide broad biological domain knowledge and biological interpretation. Mine the existing biological-science literature for new conceptual angles on the problem, propose appropriate solutions, and provide relevant papers or links.
